Program Eligibility

The DoD Mentor-Protégé Program is designed to foster business relationships between prime contractors (mentors) and small businesses (protégés). This program provides developmental assistance to enhance the capabilities of protégé firms.

To participate as a mentor, organizations must meet specific eligibility requirements and demonstrate a commitment to developing their protégé's capabilities through various forms of assistance.
